Do's and Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student   
 
Do’s
* Think before you post — once something is online, it’s hard to take back. 
* Keep your personal information private and use privacy settings wisely. 
* Double-check information before sharing anything, especially news. 
* Present yourself in a positive and professional way online. 
* Be kind and respectful when interacting with others.

Don’ts
* Don’t share too much personal information publicly. 
* Don’t post anything offensive, harmful, or inappropriate. 
* Don’t get involved in online arguments, bullying, or trolling. 
* Don’t spread rumours or fake information without checking facts. 
* Don’t spend so much time on social media that it affects your studies or well-being.
